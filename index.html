The Evolution of Aerodynamics in Modern Aviation

Fluid dynamics plays a critical role in the development of sustainable aviation. By analyzing the Reynolds number and boundary layer separation, engineers can minimize drag significantly. Recent studies in 2025 have shown that winglet modifications can reduce fuel consumption by up to 12%.

Understanding Bernoulli's principle is essential for Class 12 physics students aiming to grasp the fundamentals of lift generation.
